Section structure

How the page is positioned

This concept is designed to feel credible, modern, and useful for traders looking for a fast market overview without making misleading claims.

01

Hero with market promise

Lead with the value proposition: current prices, ranked setups, and a fast read on what is moving now in forex markets.

02

Snapshot dashboard preview

Show a live-style panel with major pairs and gold so visitors instantly understand the product before scrolling.

03

Ranked scanner outputs

Separate bullish and bearish opportunities. Include score, price, setup context, and a short rationale rather than “buy now” language.

04

Method transparency

Explain that rankings come from trend, momentum, volatility, and key-level alignment so the tool feels informative rather than magical.

05

Responsible framing

Repeat that it is a scanner for market information and trade preparation, not a promise of returns or personalized financial advice.

06

Clear next action

Invite users to review the board, confirm with their own process, and use alerts or a dashboard login if this becomes a real product.

Important: PulseFX Scanner is presented as a market information and setup-ranking interface. It is not investment advice, does not guarantee results, and should not replace your own analysis, confirmation, or risk management. Prices and setup scores shown here are illustrative for concept review.
On-page copy ideas

FAQ / trust-building copy

These sections make the concept feel polished and reduce the risk of overstating what the dashboard can do.

How are opportunities ranked?

Each setup can be scored using a blend of price momentum, trend structure, volatility conditions, and distance from meaningful support or resistance areas.

Does this tell me what to trade?

No. The dashboard is best positioned as a scanner that helps you prioritize charts and spot conditions worth reviewing more closely.

Why focus on forex first?

FX majors create a clear, liquid starting point for a scanner product. Gold can be added as an optional market for users who want broader macro exposure.

Can this work as a landing page?

Yes. This single page is structured to work as a homepage, concept mockup, demo page, or a lightweight GitHub Pages deployment for stakeholder review.
