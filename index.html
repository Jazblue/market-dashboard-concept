How it works

The update script computes yesterday's high/low from the most recent fully closed daily candle, then ranks setups around rejection, reclaim, breakout, and hold.

  • Only structured market data should feed this page.
  • PDH / PDL gives cleaner invalidation than vague signal pages.
  • Hourly refresh is enough for this style without overcomplicating it.

Risk / disclaimer

  • This dashboard is informational and educational. It is not financial advice.
  • PDH / PDL reactions fail often enough that every setup still needs defined invalidation.
  • Do not risk money you cannot afford to lose.
  • If the data feed breaks, the update workflow should preserve the last known good dataset rather than publishing bad output.
